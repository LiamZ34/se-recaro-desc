Recaro Expert M

The flexible EXPERT

If flexibility is top of your list, look no further! The modular design of the RECARO Expert adapts to different body sizes. It’s available with S and M seat cushions that vary in the length of the seat surface and the height and contours of the side bolsters. Shorter drivers will find that the short seat cushion and flat side bolsters make getting in and out a breeze (S version). Adjustable backrest side bolsters come as standard, rounding off the perfect body support. Feel your driving performance soar!
