Recaro Classic Pole Position (ABE)

Putting the class INTO CLASSIC

RECARO took the traditional design of classic-look seats and brought it up to date to create the RECARO Classic Pole Position (ABE). This Classic version of our road-legal RECARO Pole Position (ABE) racing shell, developed from the RECARO shell for the Porsche 964 RS, means you no longer have to choose between authentic appearance and modern safety standards. With its timeless shape and classic vintage covers, this seat will look at home in even the most sporty of vehicle interiors.
